Install Our App on Your Mobile Device

Get faster access and better experience

For Android Users

  1. Open Chrome on your Android device
  2. Visit comeondost.web.app (or your current site)
  3. Tap the three dots menu (â‹®) in the upper right corner
  4. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  5. Tap "Install" or "Add" on the prompt
  6. Find the app icon on your home screen
Note: The exact steps might vary slightly depending on your Android version and Chrome version.

For iPhone/iPad Users

  1. Open Safari on your iOS device
  2. Visit comeondost.web.app (or your current site)
  3. Tap the Share icon (rectangle with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
  4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  5. On the next screen, you can rename the app if desired
  6. Tap "Add" in the upper right corner
  7. Find the app icon on your home screen
Note: This feature requires iOS 11.3 or later.

Troubleshooting

If you don't see the installation option:

  • Make sure you're using the correct browser (Chrome for Android, Safari for iOS)
  • Check that you've fully loaded the website
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear your browser cache and try again
